Key Takeaways

Key takeaways from the Crazy 8's are:

1) Organizing the elements onto the page so they are easy to digest and not overwhelming
2) Creating a visual approach to displaying stats to make it accessable to new audiences
3) Social aspect of the app would be a key feature and would be based around that

The Crazy 8's process was good in identifying keep aspects of a potential user flow to understand what were the key features of the app that I wanted to highlight. The Crazy 8's also helped me with looking at visual styles I wanted to execute with the app, and allow me to reflect on not only my own style, but the styles of my class.
